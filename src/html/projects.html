GyroSnake

GyroSnake is a snake game that is played on a dot matrix display. It runs on an ATmega328 (an atmel chip) and is controlled by a gyroscope. The design I am implementing is one that incorporates fundamental concepts in real time operating systems such as event queues and task queues.
