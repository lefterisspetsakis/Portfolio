This past term, I worked for Specialty Data Systems Inc., a company which writes software for radio companies across Canada and the US. At SDS, I refactored AR Reports and redesigned many of its components to make the project faster, easier to use, and simpler to maintain. Previously, I worked at Saint Elizabeth and before that, at Rave Media Inc. as a Software Engineer, where we developed an automated mashup maker. It takes any two of your favourite hit songs from YouTube and automatically analyzes them, cuts them up, and rearranges them in a musical way that showcases the best bits of each.

I have worked on MIR systems (Music Information Retrieval) while developing algorithms that do beat detection, musical segmentation, key detection, and more. I have also dealt with RTOS (Real-Time Operating Systems) while building the GyroSnake game. This is a personal project of mine and it uses multiplexing to display 35 LEDs using only 6 output pins.

I have also done some web development, creating a little JavaScript game, and this website. This website is written mostly from scratch and incorporates Bootstrap to make it enjoyable on any device. The JavaScript game is a side scrolling jumping game that is hosted on my server.

Outside of engineering, I run a handmade pen business where I craft pens from blanks of wood on a lathe. Feel free to check out my work at spetsakispens.weebly.com. I am a person who enjoys creating things very much, a person who enjoys making others happy, and a person who enjoys playing the trumpet. I have played in many venues across Toronto and in Greece, and have played in many semi-professional orchestras and jazz/funk ensembles. I also have a younger (bigger) brother who plays football. My parents are from Greece and we go back regularly to visit our grandparents and family. I also enjoy cars and drive one with a manual transmission.
