About

RaveDJ is a web app that takes any two of your favorite songs and turns them into a mashup. It uses complicated AI algorithms that analyse the songs and put them back together in the most musical way possible. To try it out, visit rave.dj


Role

I worked for 8 months along with 4-6 other team members to design the algorithms behind RaveDJ. I developed the initial versions of the beat detection algorithm and co-developed the initial similarity algorithms.
